Class Policies.
Clear guidelines for a smooth learning experience
1. Payment Policy ›

Payment is required in advance before each session or as a monthly block booking.

• UK students: Payment via bank transfer (BACS) or PayPal in GBP (£)

• India students: Payment via UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay in INR (₹)

• Block bookings of 4+ sessions receive priority scheduling

• Receipts are issued for all payments within 24 hours

2. Cancellation Policy ›

We understand that life happens! Here's our fair cancellation policy:

• 48+ hours notice: Full refund or free reschedule

• 24–48 hours notice: 50% credit toward next session

• Less than 24 hours: Session is charged in full

• Tutor-cancelled sessions are always fully refunded or rescheduled at no cost

3. Late Policy ›

• Tutor will wait 10 minutes.

• If the student does not join, the session is counted as cancelled and is chargeable.

4. Rescheduling Policy ›

Sessions can be rescheduled with sufficient notice:

• Free reschedule with 48+ hours notice (up to 2 times per month)

• Rescheduled sessions must take place within the same calendar month

• Use the calendar on this page to check available slots before requesting a reschedule

• Email artofsciencing@gmail.com or WhatsApp us to reschedule

5. Class Conduct ›

We aim to create a safe, respectful, and focused learning environment:

• Students should be in a quiet space with minimal distractions during sessions

• Camera and microphone must be working and switched on

• Students should have relevant materials ready before the session begins

• Disruptive behaviour may result in session termination without refund

• Tutors reserve the right to decline continued sessions if conduct policies are repeatedly violated

6. Tutor Responsibilities ›

Our tutors are committed to delivering a high-quality learning experience:

• Tutors will be punctual and fully prepared for every session

• Sessions will be tailored to the student's level and learning needs

• Tutors will provide feedback and progress updates to parents regularly

• Any cancellation by the tutor will be communicated at least 24 hours in advance

• Tutors will maintain a professional and encouraging environment at all times

• Tutors will keep all student information strictly confidential

7. Parent Responsibilities ›

Parents play an important role in supporting their child's learning journey:

• Ensure the student is ready and logged in at the scheduled time

• Provide a quiet, distraction-free environment for sessions

• Ensure the student has a working device with camera and microphone

• Notify the tutor in advance of any absences or changes

• Ensure payments are made on time as agreed

• Communicate any concerns or feedback promptly to the tutor or via artofsciencing@gmail.com

8. Test & Assessment Policy ›

Regular mini-assessments help track student progress and identify areas for improvement:

• A short topic check may be given at the start or end of selected sessions to gauge understanding

• Practice papers and past exam questions will be used for GCSE, A-Level, and Class 10/12 students

• Tests are low-pressure and formative — their purpose is to guide teaching, not to grade students

• Results and feedback will be shared with parents/guardians as part of the monthly progress update

• Students are encouraged to attempt all questions and ask for help — mistakes are part of learning!

• For students preparing for JEE / NEET / GCSE / A-Level exams, targeted timed practice will be incorporated into sessions

• No test will be given without prior notice to the student and parent
